Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa is a staggering 828 metres (2,716.5 feet) tall and contains over 160 stories. This tower is the tallest free standing building in the world and the biggest attraction in Dubai. The Burj Khalifa contains offices, retail space, residential units, and a Giorgio Armani hotel. A Y-shaped floor plan maximizes views of the Arabian Gulf. At ground level, the skyscraper is surrounded by green space, water features, and pedestrian-friendly boulevards.

Tours: From 1:30 to past 6:00

Cost: Tours starting from 125 AED (34.03 US Dollars)

Address: Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Blvd, Downtown Dubai - Dubai - United Arab Emirates

Dubai Museum

The Dubai Museum is the oldest standing building in Dubai and contains life-size dioramas to illustrate the lifestyle of people living in the times before the discovery of oil. The museum accurately represents many traditional arab housing and traditions. The museum also contains artifacts from African and Asian countries as well and welcomes over 1 million visitors each year.

Private tours range from 175 AED and up (47.64 US Dollars)

Hours: 8:30 am to 2:30 pm

Address: Opp. Grand Mosque, Near Arabian Court Hotel - Al Fahidi St - Dubai - United Arab Emirates

Jumeirah Mosque

Known as the most photographed mosque in Dubai, the Jumeirah Mosque is a popular tourist attraction. The mosque displays beautiful architecture and is dedicated to promoting a cultural understanding amongst non-muslims being that this mosque is the only mosque in the UAE that is open to non-muslims

Costs: Tours Mon to Thurs. at 10:00 pm 180 AED and up (49 US Dollars)

Hours: Open Sat. to Thurs. from 9:00am to 5:00 pm

Address: Jumeirah Beach Road - Dubai - United Arab Emirates
