Madison Square Garden

Famous for its many concerts and sporting events (New York Knicks and New York Rangers), the Madison Square Garden is a key tourist attraction in New York. If your visiting through the months of November to April, check out the New York Knicks led by Carmelo Anthony. If you’re visiting from October to April, you can also check out the New York Rangers. At all times, there are many concerts and other events as well.

Address: 4 Pennsylvania Plaza, New York, NY 10001

Empire State Building

The Empire State Building is famous for its breathtaking views of New York City. The Empire State Building has an observation deck and many telescopes for great vision.

Price: $30

Address: 350 5th Avenue, New York, NY 10118

Central Park

Designed by Frederick Law Olmstead in 1858, Central Park has been one of New York’s most monumental attractions for years. In Central Park, you can experience the breathtaking 55-acre Great Lawn, enjoy the ice-skating rinks, baseball fields, tennis courts, jogging, cycling, fishing, and in-line skating. They also have the famous Central Park Zoo for family fun.

Address: Center of Manhattan, New York City

Trump Tower

Famous for its creator, the outspoken Donald Trump, Trump Towers in New York is one of New York’s best and most pricey 5 star hotels. For a luxurious feel, visit Trump International Hotel and Tower New York.

This is the iconic New York that so many visitors imagine before they visit - spectacular skyscrapers like the Chrysler Building and Empire State Building, iconic public buildings like Grand Central Terminal and the New York Public Library, and the non-stop hustle and bustle of the city streets. Fifth Avenue is a shopper's paradise where you can find every kind of store imaginable, with fashion brands ranging from the affordable to the ultra-fancy.

Address: 1 Central Park West, New York, NY 10023

Casablanca Hotel Times Square

As a 4-star hotel in the heart of New York City, the Casablanca Hotel Times Square combines great location with reasonable pricing (around $250 a night). The hotel is well-known for its great Moroccan decorating and its complementary breakfast in its private lounge.

Located in New York’s iconic Central Park, visitors will have very easy access to the great shopping, dining, and attractions around New York from the Casablanca Hotel Times Square.

Restaurants

  • Daniel
  • Colicchio & Sons
  • Faicco's Pork Store

Daniel

This pricey, Manhattan French cuisine offers all of the formalities and service of fine dining in New York City including a great wine collection. The price may run about $25 to $100 dollars though.

Colicchio & Sons

Colicchio & Sons is very famous for its dishes such as foie gras & sea salt bread and its exceptional service. Located in the heart of New York City and as a luxury dining location, Colicchio & Sons is one of New York’s best.

Faicco's Pork Store

Located in Manhattan, Faicco’s Pork Store is extremely famous for its great Italian cuisine and especially its sandwiches. They mostly serve premium deli sandwiches for about $10.
