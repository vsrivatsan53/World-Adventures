Eiffel Tower

This Staple of the City is a must see for all visitors. A marvelous feat of Engineering, it provides an unparalleled view of the city. There is nothing like this structure in the world and thus must be a part of your plan when visiting the city.

Hours: 9:30 am - 11:00 pm

Cost: $48

Address: 5 avenue Anatole France, 75007 Paris, France

Phone Number: +33 892 70 12 39

Musee de l'Orangerie

A Wonderful art museum, housing many masterpieces. A must see for visitors to the city, encasing a large potion the abundant creativity found in the city.

Hours: 9:00am- 6:00pm

Price: Starting from 9 EU (10.44 US Dollars)

Address: Jardin des Tuileries - Cote Seine, 75001 Paris, France (Louvre / Place Vendôme)

Phone Number: 33 1 44 77 80 07

Musee d'Orsay

A must see museum in Paris. These Building houses some of the Greatest works of Impressionism in the City if not the wold. A marvelous structure in its own right houses art on the outside and in.

Hours: 9:30 am - 6:00 pm

Price: 11 EU (12.75 US Dollars)

Address: 1, rue de la Legion d'Honneur, 75007 Paris, France

Phone Number: +33 (0)140 49 48 14

Epicure

Epicure, Le Bristol's triple Michelin-starred gastronomic restaurant, is located next to the hotel's magnificent French-style garden, and its bay windows allow diners to make the most of this lovely setting. Architect Pierre-Yves Rochon, in collaboration with Mrs Maja Oetker, has transformed the hotel's former summer restaurant into an exceptional, light-filled dining room, dedicated to the art of living, and the pleasure of savouring Eric Frechon's cuisine.

Address: 112 rue du Faubourg Saint-Honore, 75008 Paris, France

Cost: $15 to $46

Hours:

Sun - Sat 7:00 am - 10:30 am; 12:00 pm - 2:00 pm; 7:00 pm - 10:00 pm

Phone Number: +33 (0)1 53 43 43 40

Seb'on

A desire to feast? Seb'on is the way to go! This small restaurant located in the heart of the Montmartre district, in the street of Orsel offers a variety of delicacies from the kitchen. Seb'on for example, offers fresh lasagne, tagliatelle with squid ink and homemade dessert, roasted apricots has verbena "garden" and a foam mascarpone. A wonderfu dining area for everyone!

Address: 62 rue d'Orsel, 75018 Paris, France

Phone Number: +33 1 42 59 74 32

A L'endroit
